Welcome

Hello, My name is Joe Mendez-Hughes and I am a dedicated DevOps Engineer with 7 years of professional experience in architecting CI/CD pipelines, working within cloud environments such as Azure and AWS, and building secure and reliable Kubernetes clusters and optimized Docker containers. I rely heavily on automation tools such as Ansible and Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) tools such as Terraform, to create an environment ideal for fast iteration and seamless deployments.

My main goal as a DevOps Engineer is to streamline the development process as much as possible, so teams can focus on the task-at-hand. I automate as much as I can and utilize IaC, so whether it's containers, the CI/CD pipeline, or repositories, these are taken care of and not a roadblock within the overall development process. This leaves the teams to focus on their tasks, and not worry about all the other things going on within the software development cycle.
